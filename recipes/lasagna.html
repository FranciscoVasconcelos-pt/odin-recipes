Homemade Lasagna

Delicious, moisty and simple lasagna to make at home!

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna pasta
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Beef and Sausage
  • Pasta sauce
  • Garlic
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Spices
  • Egg
  • Parsely
  • Tomato sauce and onion

Step by step:

  1. Boil the paste and prepare your meat sauce!
  2. Combine your cheese mixture and prepare to layer the lasagna pasta!
  3. After doing the layers, proceed to bake it for 45m!
