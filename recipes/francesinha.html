Homemade francesinha

Delicious, moisty and simple francesinha to make at home!

Ingredients:

  • Porto wine, white wine and beer
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Beef and Sausage
  • Pasta sauce
  • Garlic
  • Spices
  • Egg
  • olive oil
  • Tomato sauce and onion

Step by step:

  1. Prepare the mixture of the liquids and boil it!
  2. Season the meat and toast the buns, and layer it!
  3. After doing the layers, proceed to bake it for 20m! and add the boiling sauce!
